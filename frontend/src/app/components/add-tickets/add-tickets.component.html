Tickets & price

Issuing more tickets for an event can lead to increased revenue and higher attendance, expanding your reach and potential audience. However, it also carries risks such as overcrowding, resource strain, and potential negative feedback from attendees.

On the other hand, issuing fewer tickets can create an intimate experience, ensure a controlled environment, and increase the perceived value of tickets. However, it may also result in missed revenue opportunities, exclusivity concerns, and the risk of underbooking the event.
